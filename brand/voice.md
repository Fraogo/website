# Fraogo Brand Voice

## Who Fraogo Is

A Nigerian company that takes care of the hard parts — sourcing, shipping, vendor coordination.
Reliable, professional, and direct. Not flashy, not corporate. Human but competent.

---

## Tone

| Situation | Tone |
|-----------|------|
| Marketing headings | Bold, direct, confident |
| Service descriptions | Clear, factual, no hype |
| Process / how it works | Step-by-step, reassuring |
| Error messages | Calm, helpful, never alarming |
| Admin / internal | Professional, concise |

---

## Voice Principles

**Direct, not clever.**
Say exactly what you mean. "We source products for you" not "Empowering procurement excellence."

**Confident, not boastful.**
Don't claim awards, rankings, or superlatives we can't back up. The work speaks.

**Human, not corporate.**
Write like a real person. Short sentences. No jargon. No buzzwords.

**Honest about what we are.**
We are a young, registered Nigerian company (RC8967311). We don't pretend to be a global conglomerate. That's fine — our clients value the personal touch.

---

## Writing Rules

- Use **"we"** and **"you"** — conversational, not distant
- Use **Nigerian English** — no Americanisms that feel out of place
- **No exclamation marks** on marketing copy — confidence doesn't shout
- **No fake urgency** — "Limited time!" type copy is banned
- **No unverified claims** — "trusted by thousands", "industry-leading", "Nigeria's #1" are all banned unless we have real data
- CTAs: action-first — **"Start an Order"**, **"Contact Us"**, **"Get in touch"** — not "Discover More"

---

## Headline Patterns

Good:
- "Procurement. Logistics. General Services — Done in Nigeria."
- "Three services. One partner."
- "From sourcing to delivery, we handle it."

Avoid:
- "Revolutionising Nigerian Logistics!"
- "Your Trusted Procurement Partner™"
- "Seamlessly connecting businesses to world-class solutions"

---

## The RC Number

`RC8967311` is a real, verifiable fact. It appears:
- In the footer (prose, not a badge)
- On the About page (in the brand story)

It does **not** appear as a trust badge, icon tile, or decorative element.
It is stated plainly as a fact about who we are — nothing more.
