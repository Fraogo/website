# Fraogo Brand Voice

A registered Nigerian company that takes care of the hard parts — sourcing, shipping, vendor coordination. Reliable, professional, direct. Human but competent — not flashy, not corporate.

## Tone by Situation

| Situation | Tone |
|-----------|------|
| Marketing headings | Bold, direct, confident |
| Service descriptions | Clear, factual, no hype |
| Process / how it works | Step-by-step, reassuring |
| Error messages | Calm, helpful, never alarming |
| Admin / internal | Professional, concise |

## Principles

- **Direct, not clever** — "We source products for you", not "Empowering procurement excellence."
- **Confident, not boastful** — no awards/rankings/superlatives we can't back up.
- **Human, not corporate** — short sentences, no jargon, no buzzwords.
- **Honest about what we are** — a young, registered Nigerian company (RC8967311). The personal touch is the value.

## Writing Rules

- Use **"we"** and **"you"** — conversational.
- Nigerian English — no out-of-place Americanisms.
- No exclamation marks on marketing copy. No fake urgency ("Limited time!").
- No unverified claims — "trusted by thousands", "industry-leading", "Nigeria's #1" are banned without real data.
- CTAs are action-first: **"Start an Order"**, **"Contact Us"**, **"Get in touch"** — not "Discover More".

## Headlines

✅ "Procurement. Logistics. General Services — Done in Nigeria." · "Three services. One partner." · "From sourcing to delivery, we handle it."

❌ "Revolutionising Nigerian Logistics!" · "Your Trusted Procurement Partner™" · "Seamlessly connecting businesses to world-class solutions"

## The RC Number

`RC8967311` is real and verifiable. It appears in the footer (prose) and the About page (brand story) — stated plainly as fact. **Never** as a trust badge, icon tile, or decorative element.
